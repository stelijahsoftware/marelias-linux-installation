# Laptop disadvantages:
1. wrist aches, neck aches (only when it is not standing up). can be solved by connecting to a monitor.
2. limited battery life time (a few years). Unnecessary.
3. connecting an HDD with SATA for quick backups, especially 3.5 inch HDD. Unnecessary.

Dependence on electricity and pc (solve using kindle reader, or a reader that can add annotations to pdfs, not just save them to a txt file).

# Laptop advantages (Lenovo ThinkPad X270):
1. portability
2. no accidental electricity shutdown worries
3. instant sleep/wakeup

pdf books advantage: Searching & size/weight/space (more freedom)
